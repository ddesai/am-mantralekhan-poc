Help Swaminarayan Mahamantra Lekhan

  • You can perform Mantra lekhan by selecting either the Tapping or Typing option from the Menu.
  • You can play Swaminarayan Dhun by tapping on the play button in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  • In horizontal view, you can position the Tapping Button either on the Left or Right.
  • When app is opened upon availability of internet, your mantra count will update automatically.
  • If you clear app data or uninstall the app from your device, the un-synchronized data will be lost.
